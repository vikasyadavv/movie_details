## 📱 Pro Implementation Tips

1. **Start Small**: Add 2-3 new fields first, test, then add more
2. **Test Formatting**: Long text can be hard to read on mobile
3. **Create Variations**: Different shortcuts for different purposes
4. **Use Folders**: Organize movie-related shortcuts together
5. **Share & Backup**: Export shortcuts for friends or backup

## 🚨 Things to Watch Out For

- **Empty Fields**: Some movies may not have all data (especially older films)
- **Text Length**: Too much info can be overwhelming on mobile
- **API Limits**: Free tier has 1000 requests/day
- **Special Characters**: Some movie titles may have encoding issues
- **Variable Names**: Always use variable selector, never type manually

Choose the fields that matter most to you and gradually build up your shortcut! 🎬
